✨ Habita Tutorial

Welcome to Habita! 👋

Let's take a quick tour to help you get the most out of your productivity journey. This tutorial will take about 2 minutes.

Step 1 of 7

Your Inbox 📥

This is where new tasks live. Add tasks here first, then organize them into the matrix to prioritize by time and energy.

💡 Tip: Click "Add Task" to create your first task!
Step 2 of 7

The Ike Matrix 📊

Organize your tasks into 4 quadrants based on energy and time required:

  • Do First: High energy, short time - your priorities
  • Schedule: High energy, long time - plan these
  • Delegate: Low energy, short time - quick wins
  • Eliminate: Low energy, long time - reconsider these
Step 3 of 7

Build Habits 🌟

Track daily habits with the +/− buttons. No streak shaming here—just gentle progress tracking.

  • Each habit has a daily target (e.g., 8 glasses of water)
  • The progress bar shows your daily completion
  • Habits reset daily for fresh starts
    • Step 4 of 7

    Calendar Overview 📅

    See scheduled tasks and completed habits at a glance. Green shows completed days, blue shows scheduled tasks.

    Step 5 of 7

    Track Your Progress 📈

    The Progress tab shows three views:

    • Completion Table: Daily, weekly, and monthly habit success rates
    • Distribution Chart: How many tasks are in each status
    • Trend Graph: Your last 7 days of task completion
    Step 6 of 7

    You're All Set! 🎉

    Quick tips to get started:

    • 💾 Your data saves automatically to your browser
    • 🌙 Toggle dark mode in Settings
    • 📥 Export your data anytime for backup
    • 🎯 Start small—even one task makes a difference
    Ready to build better habits? Let's go! 💪
